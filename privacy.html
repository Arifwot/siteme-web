  • Beta/test data: data created during early access, alpha/beta tests, or developer previews.

9. Security & End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)

WotPan uses technical and organizational measures (industry-standard encryption, secure servers) to protect data. For private chats, we provide **end-to-end encryption** which means message content is cryptographically accessible only to the communicating users (not WotPan). However, certain metadata (timestamps, sender/recipient identifiers, message size) may be processed by us to route and deliver messages, prevent abuse, and provide features. While we use strong safeguards, no system is perfectly secure.
